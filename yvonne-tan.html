Yvonne Tan is the Group Managing Director of JonDavidson, bringing over 25 years of experience in executive search and human capital consulting across Southeast Asia.

Her deep industry knowledge spans financial services, technology, consumer, and industrial sectors. She has successfully placed C-suite and senior management professionals across Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, and Vietnam.

Yvonne is passionate about building long-term partnerships with clients and candidates alike, anchored in trust, integrity, and deep market expertise.
