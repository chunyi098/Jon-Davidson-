WHO WE ARE

Our Story

We are a team of industry specialists who share a belief that a Company's success is fuelled by having the right talents in the right job functions. The talent must also have a good cultural fit and shared core values with the Company, which is pivotal to job satisfaction. This belief forms the basis in our search for executive talent for our clients.

Recognizing our collective passion to help businesses develop a competent and effective workforce, we came together in May 2007. Our own diverse talents are synergized to bring an energetic dynamism in fulfilling the recruitment needs of our clients, and the career aspirations of our candidates.

Our Values

Our clients are meaningfully engaged with us, whether it is in executive search, consultancy or training. Each person is treated as a unique individual with different motivations and aspirations. They tell us their stories and challenges and work in partnership with us to achieve not only the Company's success, but the individual's success as well.

World Wide Reach

We have grown our executive search and consulting network from Singapore to Asia Pacific including China, India and Vietnam. Through the ENEX network, JonDavidson Group is connected globally.

Local Expertise

Deep understanding of Southeast Asian markets, cultures, and talent dynamics — giving our clients an edge that global generalists cannot replicate.

True Engagement

Every individual we work with receives personalised, thoughtful engagement — because real relationships are the foundation of lasting success.

GLOBAL NETWORK

Connect to our Global Expertise — ENEX

We are the South East Asia (SEA) accredited partner of ENEX global executive search organization. The ENEX Partner network has coverage in four continents & is represented in 40 countries.

The strategy to our success is based on our proven ability to serve our clients with unparalleled speed and refined accuracy in the area of talent acquisition and commitment. Our team members are skilled and experienced professionals from diverse backgrounds who provide highly personalised advice and work together to deliver consistent results for both our clients and candidates.
